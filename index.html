about me

hi, i'm jingkai

This website was created with love, as part of the POSC103: World Politics course. As a Computer Science student with a passion for design and 3D modeling, I've built this site to reflect the theme of my own room. This is special, as I wanted to illustrate how world politics and global events shape the daily lives of individuals like myself. This highlights the importance of having a deep understanding of current affairs in today's complex, dynamic, and often chaotic world.

about me

My fascination with global affairs stems from my time in New York, where I worked at a tech startup positioned at the intersection of AI and public policy. Being there allowed me to witness firsthand how U.S. policy directly influences the global political landscape. I enrolled in this course to gain deeper insights into these international dynamics and to better understand the forces that move our world.
